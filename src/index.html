Wanderer

This is an editorial design for Wanderer magazine featuring unconventional travel destinations. The inside spreads are tailored to resemble the look and feel of Industrial Manchester while highlighting their history and attractions.

Nest

Borrowing from the folklore of storks delivering babies, I composed a story to build upon the Nest brand and created packaging for chocolate with mothers of newborns in mind. The story goes…

Nothing will ever be the same again as a mother-to-be prepares to sacrifice her chocolate addiction so her newborn could have a healthy beginning. Noticing the cries of these mothers-to-be, storks from all over the world set out on an epic journey to find the best cocoa beans from the Amazon region. After successfully harvesting the cocoa beans, they deliver them to a skilled chocolatier who then carefully crafts them into fine white chocolate. Mothers with newborns will never have to sacrifice again.

Student work created using Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop
